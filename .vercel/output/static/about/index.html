Who We Are

More than training — a movement for change

ActionPath Africa is a Pan-African livelihood training, consultancy, and capacity building organisation committed to transforming the trajectories of communities living at the intersection of poverty, climate vulnerability, conflict, and disease.

We work where others struggle to — in post-conflict zones, drought-stricken landscapes, urban slums, and HIV-affected households — delivering practical, context-specific programs that give individuals not just skills, but agency, dignity, and economic pathways.

Our model is built on three pillars: rigorous curriculum design, participatory community engagement, and sustainable institutional partnerships. Together these pillars ensure our programs outlast donor funding, embed within local systems, and create ripple effects across entire communities.

Community-owned, market-linked livelihood programs
Accredited training aligned to national qualifications frameworks
Psychosocial support integrated into all programs
Gender-responsive and youth-inclusive by design

Our Mission

To empower individuals with practical skills and knowledge that foster sustainable livelihoods, uphold integrity in every engagement, and champion inclusivity by creating opportunities for all, especially marginalized communities.

Our Vision

To be a leading consultancy that transforms lives by building resilient, self-reliant communities where empowerment, integrity, and inclusivity drive lasting social and economic progress.

Our Approach

Holistic, evidence-based, and rooted in community realities. We combine practical skills training with entrepreneurial development, psychosocial support, and systemic institutional partnerships — ensuring impact that lasts beyond donor cycles.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

Three principles that shape every program, partnership, and decision we make.

01

Empowerment

We focus on equipping trainees with practical skills, confidence, and self-reliance so they can sustainably improve their livelihoods. This means our work has a lasting impact beyond training.

02

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all engagements. This means we build trust with trainees, partners, and communities, ensuring our work is credible and dependable.

03

Inclusivity

We design and deliver training programs that are accessible to diverse groups, including marginalized communities, women, and youth. This ensures equal opportunity and broadens the reach of our impact.

Our Journey(SAMPLE)

A Decade of Milestones

From a bold idea in Nairobi to a continental force for change.

2015

ActionPath Africa Founded

Established in Nairobi by a team of development practitioners committed to redefining livelihood training in East Africa.

2017

First Regional Expansion

Launched operations in Rwanda and Ethiopia, extending our reach to post-conflict and drought-affected communities.

2019

10,000 Beneficiaries Milestone

Crossed the landmark of 10,000 lives directly impacted through livelihood programs, vocational training, and economic empowerment.

2021

Strategic Partnership with UNDP

Formalized a multi-year partnership with UNDP Africa to deliver climate-resilient agriculture programs across six counties in Kenya.

2023

Expanded to the rest of Africa

Broadened our continental footprint, delivering programs across East, West, and Southern Africa.

2025

15,000+ Lives Transformed

Reached our most ambitious milestone yet — over 15,000 individuals transformed, 250+ projects delivered, and 28 communities served across Africa.
