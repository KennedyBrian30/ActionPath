The People Behind the Work

Meet the Team

ActionPath Africa is built on lived experience, deep relationships, and a genuine commitment to communities that are too often underserved. Here are the people who make the work possible.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with practical skills and knowledge that foster sustainable livelihoods, uphold integrity in every engagement, and champion inclusivity by creating opportunities for all, especially marginalized communities.

Our Vision

To be a leading consultancy that transforms lives by building resilient, self-reliant communities where empowerment, integrity, and inclusivity drive lasting social and economic progress.

Our People

A small team with deep reach

ActionPath Africa operates as a lean, experienced team that punches well above its size. Each member brings a distinct set of skills and relationships that combine to deliver programs in some of the most complex livelihood environments in East Africa. We work with a network of associates, community facilitators, and partner organisations to extend our reach without losing the quality and care that define our approach.

George Omondi Awuor

Founder & CEO, ActionPath Africa

"Building learning experiences that create real impact."

George Omondi Awuor has spent over a decade designing and implementing training programs across East Africa. He has worked alongside organisations including VSO International, ActionAid, Mercy Corps, Tiko Africa, ACE Africa, Jobortunity, and Sibusiso Foundation, building a body of work that spans some of the most complex livelihood contexts on the continent.

He specialises in curriculum development, youth empowerment, facilitation, behaviour change, community engagement, and workforce readiness training. His work has supported thousands of youth, women, facilitators, and health workers in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, and Kakuma Refugee Camp to build practical skills, confidence, and opportunity.

As the lead at ActionPath Africa, George champions community-driven learning approaches that help NGOs, CBOs, social enterprises, and institutions create sustainable impact in underserved communities.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at ActionPath Africa.

Empowerment

We focus on equipping trainees with practical skills, confidence, and self-reliance so they can sustainably improve their livelihoods. This means our work has a lasting impact beyond training.

Integrity

We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical practices in all engagements. This means we build trust with trainees, partners, and communities, ensuring our work is credible and dependable.

Inclusivity

We design and deliver training programs that are accessible to diverse groups, including marginalized communities, women, and youth. This ensures equal opportunity and broadens the reach of our impact.
