Organization Documentation

Organization documentation is the backbone of sustainability, accountability, and growth. An organization, CBO, or social enterprise with passionate members but poor documentation will struggle to attract funding, survive leadership changes, pass audits, or even remember what it decided last month. At ActionPath Africa, we equip participants with knowledge to create, organize, store, and use the essential records that keep a group legitimate, transparent, and efficient from day one.
