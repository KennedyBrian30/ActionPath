Risk Analysis & Sustainability

Risk analysis and sustainability are what separate short-lived youth initiatives from organizations and enterprises that endure. Many youth-led CBOs and startups fail not because they had bad ideas, but because they didn't see risks coming, or they built everything around a single grant that ended. At ActionPath Africa, we train organizations in risk analysis and sustainability to identify what could go wrong, plan for it, and build systems that outlast any single funding source, leader, or opportunity.
