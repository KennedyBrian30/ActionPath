Our Rationale

Why CBO capacity matters

Community-Based Organizations are not just service providers — they are the custodians of local trust, cultural knowledge, and community agency. When they are strong, development sticks. When they are weak, even the best-funded programs fail.

Across Africa, CBOs are implementing youth empowerment initiatives, supporting smallholder farmers, running financial literacy groups, and providing care for people living with HIV/AIDS. They tailor programs to community needs in ways that no external organization ever could.

Yet the same CBOs that deliver this impact often struggle with proposal writing, donor reporting, financial governance, and fundraising strategy. ActionPath Africa exists to close that gap — strengthening the organizational infrastructure that makes community impact sustainable.

What CBOs Do

Key Livelihood Activities We Support

ActionPath Africa supports CBOs across these four core livelihood domains — the pillars of sustainable, community-owned development.

Skills Development & Entrepreneurship

Training in tailoring, carpentry, and mechanics alongside business management and financial literacy to empower youth and women.

Agricultural Support & Food Security

Access to quality seeds, farming tools, and sustainable practices like agroforestry for community food security.

Microfinance & Savings

Training CBOs on savings group formation and interloaning structures for sustainable, community-managed income generation.

Marginalized Group Support

Targeted support for PLHIV, people with disabilities, and other vulnerable populations — ensuring dignified economic inclusion.

Our Support Package

Six ways we strengthen your CBO

A structured, comprehensive package designed to build lasting organizational capacity — from fundraising skills to accountability systems.

01

Training Workshops

Structured, interactive workshops covering proposal writing, budgeting, donor engagement, and financial reporting. We use adult learning principles and participatory methods so that skills stick long after the training ends.

Deliverables
Grant proposal writing
Budget development & tracking
Donor communication strategy
Fundraising plan templates
02

Practical Toolkits

Ready-to-use toolkits with templates, frameworks, and step-by-step guides for grant applications, financial management, and fundraising campaigns — customized to the East African donor landscape.

Deliverables
Grant application templates
Financial tracking spreadsheets
Fundraising calendar frameworks
Donor reporting checklists
03

Mentorship & Coaching

We pair CBO leaders with experienced development practitioners for one-on-one and group mentorship — providing hands-on guidance through live grant applications, board governance, and strategic planning.

Deliverables
1-on-1 executive coaching
Peer mentorship circles
Board governance advisory
Strategic plan co-development
04

Resource Mobilisation

Beyond training, we actively support CBOs to identify, pursue, and secure funding. We provide donor mapping, facilitate introductions, support joint proposals with NGO partners, and guide income-generating activities.

Deliverables
Donor database & mapping
Joint proposal partnerships
Community crowdfunding support
Income-generating enterprise design
05

Research & Donor Intelligence

We keep CBOs ahead of funding trends. Our research team tracks active donors in Kenya and East Africa, identifies thematic funding windows (climate, youth, gender), and facilitates networking at donor roundtables and conferences.

Deliverables
Active donor database access
Funding trend analysis
Networking forum facilitation
Conference & roundtable introductions
06

Accountability & M&E Systems

We help CBOs build credible monitoring, evaluation, and accountability systems that satisfy donor requirements and demonstrate real community impact — making repeat funding far more likely.

Deliverables
M&E framework design
MEARL system setup
Donor reporting templates
Impact story documentation

Built for sustainability, not dependency

Everything we do is designed to make our support unnecessary over time. Through peer learning exchanges — where CBOs share fundraising wins and lessons — and documented case studies that showcase success to donors, we build confidence and institutional memory. We actively encourage diversification of funding sources so that no CBO ever relies on a single donor or partner again.

Peer learning exchanges Case study documentation Funding diversification Adaptive strategy coaching
Our Process

From assessment to fundraising success

A clear, five-step journey that takes your CBO from where you are to where you need to be.

1

Initial Assessment

We conduct a structured capacity assessment of your CBO — governance, finance, programs, fundraising — to identify priority gaps.

2

Tailored Capacity Plan

Using assessment findings, we co-design a capacity building plan with timelines, milestones, and measurable outcomes.

3

Training & Mentorship

We deliver workshops, toolkits, and ongoing mentorship — adapting our approach to your team's learning style and context.

4

Resource Mobilisation

We support grant writing, donor introductions, and partnership development as your CBO pursues its first or next round of funding.

5

Monitoring & Learning

We track progress together, document wins, and adapt the plan based on what's working — building a culture of continuous improvement.
