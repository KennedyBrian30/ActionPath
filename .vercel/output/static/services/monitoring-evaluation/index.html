Why It Matters

Good intentions without evidence are just assumptions

Development programs spend millions of donor dollars every year without ever rigorously measuring whether they are creating the change they promise. The result: communities receive programs that feel helpful but don't actually shift outcomes — and donors fund cycles that repeat the same mistakes.

ActionPath Africa's Monitoring & Evaluation service exists to break that cycle. We design and implement M&E systems that are proportionate to program size, grounded in community realities, and rigorous enough to satisfy the most demanding donor standards.

Whether you need a full MEARL system built from scratch, an independent end-line evaluation, or support strengthening your existing data processes — we deliver evidence that is honest, actionable, and impact-focused. We also train your team so M&E capacity stays in-house long after our engagement ends.

Our Framework

The MEARL Framework — Beyond Basic M&E

We operate within a MEARL framework — Monitoring, Evaluation, Accountability, Research & Learning — that goes beyond data collection to build genuine organizational intelligence.

M
Monitoring

Continuous tracking of activities, outputs, and early outcomes — are we doing what we said we would do?

E
Evaluation

Periodic, rigorous assessment of outcomes and impact — are we achieving the change we set out to create?

A
Accountability

Transparent reporting to donors, communities, and participants — are we being honest about what is and isn't working?

R
Research

Generating evidence on what works in which contexts — contributing to the wider development knowledge base.

L
Learning

Using findings to adapt, improve, and innovate — ensuring programs evolve in response to evidence rather than assumption.

What We Offer

Our M&E Service Areas

Six integrated service areas covering the full M&E lifecycle — from design through implementation to learning and reporting.

Program Baseline Assessments

Rigorous pre-intervention data collection establishing clear benchmarks against which all program outcomes are measured — ensuring impact is real, not assumed.

Impact Evaluation Frameworks

Designing Theory of Change models, results frameworks, and logical frameworks (logframes) tailored to your program's specific objectives and donor requirements.

Real-Time Data Collection

Mobile-based data collection tools including ODK, KoboToolbox, and custom survey platforms that capture field data accurately and efficiently at scale.

Mid-Term & End-Line Evaluations

Independent, credible evaluations at program milestones — providing honest assessment of what is working, what isn't, and what should change.

Participatory Community Assessments

Community-led data processes including focus group discussions, key informant interviews, and participatory rural appraisals (PRA) that centre local voices.

Donor Reporting & Documentation

Clear, compelling impact reports that meet the standards of bilateral donors, foundations, and UN agencies — translating field data into funding-sustaining narratives.

Our Process

How We Deliver M&E Engagements

A six-stage engagement process that builds rigorous evidence while transferring M&E capacity to your organization.

01

Scoping & Design

We work with your team to understand program objectives, donor requirements, and community context — then co-design an M&E framework that is proportionate, practical, and credible.

02

Baseline Data Collection

Trained enumerators deploy mobile tools to collect baseline data from program areas — establishing the benchmark that makes all future impact claims defensible.

03

Indicator Tracking System

We build or strengthen your MEAL system — whether a simple spreadsheet dashboard or a full database — ensuring data flows from field to management reliably.

04

Mid-Term Review

At program midpoint we conduct a participatory review — assessing progress, documenting lessons, and recommending course corrections while there is still time to act.

05

End-Line Evaluation

A rigorous final assessment comparing end-line data against baseline — attributing change to the intervention and producing the evidence donors and communities deserve.

06

Learning & Reporting

We translate findings into accessible reports, case studies, and learning briefs — ensuring evidence informs your next program and contributes to the wider sector.
