Testimonials

Real stories from the communities we serve across Africa.

“Before joining ActionPath Africa's training, I often struggled to manage community disputes and express our challenges clearly in reports. The Conflict Resolution and Report Writing Skills training completely transformed my approach. I learned how to listen actively, mediate fairly, and document issues in a way that promotes understanding and accountability. Today, I can help my community resolve conflicts peacefully and prepare reports that attract support from partners. ActionPath Africa didn't just train me; they empowered me to become a bridge-builder and a voice for my people.”

Charles Lopir

Employee at the Danish Refugee Council

Kakuma Refugee Camp, Kenya

Refugee from South Sudan
