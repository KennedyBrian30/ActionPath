Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Find quick answers to common questions about our services

How can I enroll in your training programs?

You can enroll by filling out the contact form on this page, selecting 'Training Inquiry' as the reason. Our team will respond within 24-48 hours with program details, schedules, and enrollment procedures.

Do you offer online/virtual training?

Yes! We offer remote and hybrid learning options for most of our training programs. Our digital platforms make it possible to reach communities even in areas with limited infrastructure.

What is the typical duration of your training programs?

Program durations vary based on the specific training. Our workshops range from 1-day intensive sessions to 3-month comprehensive programs. Custom durations can be arranged based on organizational needs.

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Yes, all our training programs provide certificates of completion. We also work with accredited institutions to offer recognized certifications where applicable.

How can my organization partner with ActionPath Africa?

We welcome partnerships with NGOs, government agencies, foundations, and private sector organizations. Please select 'Partnership Opportunities' in the contact form, and our partnerships team will reach out.

What countries do you operate in?

Currently, we operate in Kenya, Rwanda, Ethiopia, Uganda, Tanzania, and South Sudan. We are actively expanding to other African countries. Contact us to discuss potential collaboration in your region.
